Menu

At Little Lemon, we believe in keeping things fresh and exciting. Our menu rotates seasonally to highlight the best ingredients and flavors each season has to offer. Our chefs use traditional recipes and techniques from Italy, Greece, and Turkey to create dishes that are bold, flavorful, and satisfying.

Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Chicago, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant that blends tradition with a modern twist. Our menu draws inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish flavors, creating a unique dining experience that’s both authentic and innovative. Whether you’re looking for a cozy spot to enjoy a meal with friends or a quick bite, Little Lemon welcomes you to explore the rich, bold flavors of the Mediterranean.

Little Lemon was founded by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who dreamed of sharing their love for Mediterranean cuisine with Chicago.

Our Team

Mario Draghi

Chef

Mario, one of the founders of Little Lemon, grew up in Italy and has spent years perfecting his craft in the kitchen. His dishes are rooted in family recipes passed down through generations, with just the right amount of creativity and a modern twist. Mario believes that food is a way of connecting with loved ones, and he brings this passion to every dish he creates at Little Lemon.

Adrian Newey

CMO

Adrian, on the other hand, is the marketing mind behind Little Lemon. Thanks to his vision, the restaurant has evolved from a traditional Italian eatery to a vibrant spot showcasing flavors from across the Mediterranean. Together, the brothers have created a warm, rustic space where everyone can enjoy delicious food and a welcoming atmosphere.
